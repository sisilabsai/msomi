002 — System Architecture

Three Core Layers

Msomi is built in three layers, each serving a distinct function. Together they create a complete intelligence loop.

01
🔍
Intelligence Layer
AI Brain · Context Engine
The reasoning engine. Combines market data, news sentiment, and technical context to produce human-readable analysis — not just signals, but why.
  • Claude/GPT-4 market commentary per signal
  • Sentiment analysis from financial news feeds
  • Regime detection (trending vs ranging)
  • Confidence scoring with plain-language rationale
  • Multi-timeframe confluence analysis
02
📡
Signal Layer
Detection Engine · Rules Core
The technical engine. Runs indicator combinations across multiple timeframes, scores setups 1–100, and only surfaces high-conviction opportunities.
  • EMA, RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, ATR, VWAP
  • Multi-indicator confluence scoring
  • Pattern recognition (H&S, double tops, flags)
  • Timeframe alignment checks (M5 + M15 + H1)
  • Volume confirmation filters
03
🛡️
Risk Layer
Capital Protector · Discipline Engine
The discipline engine. Hard-coded rules that protect your capital regardless of conviction level. No overrides allowed in live mode.
  • Per-trade max risk % (default: 2% of balance)
  • Daily max loss circuit breaker (default: 10%)
  • Consecutive loss streak detection & pause
  • Session quality filters (avoid low liquidity)
  • Correlation exposure limits across positions

Signal Narration

News Synthesis

📊
Regime Awareness

🪞
Trade Review
After each trade, AI reviews what happened vs prediction and logs learnings to improve future analysis.
⚠️
Risk Commentary
Flags sessions, news events, or market conditions where signal reliability is expected to be lower.

Strategy Coach
Weekly AI review of your trading history — pattern recognition on your own behavior and decisions.
Signal Module
Signal Detection Engine
The core technical brain. Watches multiple assets simultaneously and fires alerts only on high-confidence confluences.
  • 200 EMA trend filter
  • RSI divergence detection
  • MACD histogram momentum
  • Bollinger Band squeeze & breakout
  • VWAP deviation signals
  • ATR-based volatility filtering
  • Confluence scoring (1–100)
  • Configurable minimum score threshold
  • Multi-timeframe confirmation
  • Candlestick pattern recognition
  • Support & resistance zone detection
  • Chart pattern detection (H&S, flags, triangles)
Data Module
Market Data Pipeline
Ingests, normalizes, and stores real-time and historical price data across forex pairs and crypto assets.
  • WebSocket real-time price feeds
  • Multi-source aggregation (Yahoo Finance, Twelve Data, Binance API)
  • OHLCV data normalization
  • Historical data warehouse (SQLite/PostgreSQL)
  • Economic calendar feed (high-impact events)
  • Crypto on-chain data integration
  • Funding rates & open interest (crypto)
  • News feed ingestion (RSS + scraping)
  • Data gap detection & auto-healing
  • Asset watchlist management
Risk Module
Risk & Position Manager
Automated capital protection system. Calculates position sizes and enforces hard limits before any trade fires.
  • Dynamic position sizing (% of balance)
  • Per-trade max loss cap
  • Daily loss limit & auto-pause
  • Weekly drawdown monitoring
  • Win/loss streak detection
  • Consecutive loss pause (e.g. 3 losses = stop)
  • Session quality scoring
  • Correlation risk across open positions
  • Martingale strategy detection & block
  • Risk-reward ratio enforcement (min 1.5:1)
Backtest Module
Strategy Backtesting Engine
Validate any strategy across years of historical data before a single live cent is committed.
  • Full OHLCV backtesting via vectorbt
  • Walk-forward analysis
  • Monte Carlo simulation (300+ scenarios)
  • Win rate, Sharpe ratio, max drawdown metrics
  • Slippage & spread modeling
  • Multi-asset concurrent backtesting
  • Strategy parameter optimization
  • Overfitting detection
  • Comparison across strategies
  • Export results to PDF/CSV
Alerts Module
Notification & Alert System
Msomi watches the market 24/7. You get a ping only when something is worth acting on.
  • Telegram bot with rich signal messages
  • Signal card: asset, direction, score, expiry
  • AI reasoning attached to every alert
  • Risk parameters included in notification
  • News event warnings before high-impact releases
  • Daily performance summary (EOD report)
  • Circuit breaker alerts (daily loss hit)
  • Watchlist price level alerts
  • Quiet hours / DND scheduling
  • Email digest (optional)
